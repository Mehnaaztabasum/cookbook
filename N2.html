Rasgulla

Method

  1. Step 1 Make chenna and mash Boil milk. When the milk starts boiling add lemon juice. Once it is done. Strain the Chenna water. Add water to cool it. Transfer the Chenna to a plate. Mash with your palm till it becomes smooth.

  2. Step 2 Make chena balls From the Chena mixture, squeeze a little amount and make small balls. Keep aside.

  3. Step 3 Prepare the Rabdi For Rabdi, heat the milk in a kadhai on a low flame till it comes to boil. Simmer and cook till the milk reduces. While scrapping the sides of the pan continously.

  4. Step 4 Dip rasgulla in rabdi Add sugar and simmer continuously till the milk thickens. Once it is done. Transfer the rabdi to a plate. Dip the rasgulla in rabdi and keep aside till the rasgulla become soft.

  5. Step 5 Serve Once it is done. Transfer to a plate and garnish with dry fruits. Rabdi - Ragullah is Ready to serve

Ingredients

  • 1 litre milk
  • 4 strands saffron
  • sugar as required
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 litre milk
