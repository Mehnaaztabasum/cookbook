Strawberry juice

Method

  1. STEP 1 Mix the gin with the strawberries and caster sugar in a large bowl and pour into a 1.5-litre sterilised Kilner jar.

  2. STEP 2 Store in the fridge and stir every two days for three weeks. Strain though coffee filters or muslin cloth to serve.

  3. You can visit You tube link also..here click the above link to open Strawberry juice

Ingredients

  • 700ml bottle of gin
  • 400g punnet of strawberries, sliced
  • 100g caster sugar
