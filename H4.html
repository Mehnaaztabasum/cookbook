Poha

Method

  1. Step 1 Saute onions and spices Heat olive oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, dry red chilli and curry leaves. Let them splutter for a minute. Now add onions and let them saute for 1-2 minutes.

  2. Step 2 Add the veggies Now add grated cauliflower, turmeric and salt. Mix well. Add water and let it boil once.

  3. Step 3 Add diced cauliflower Now add finely diced cabbage and mix well. Lastly, add roasted peanuts and lemon juice.

  4. Step 4 Garnish and serve Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve. Make sure you try out the recipe, rate it, like it and leave your comments in the section below.

  5. You can visit You tube link also..here click the above link to open .Poha.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cauliflower, 1/2 cup onion, curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 dry red chili 1/4 cup water 1/2 cup cabbage
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • salt as required 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
