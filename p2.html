Bubble waffles

Method

  1. STEP 1 Whisk all the waffle ingredients together in a large bowl with a pinch o f salt until you have a smooth batter. Cover and leave to rest in the fridge for 1 hr, then transfer to a jug.

  2. STEP 2 Brush the bubble waffle pan with a little oil, then set over a medium heat. Once hot, pour in half the batter, filling each hole evenly. Attach the lid of the waffle pan, close and immediately flip over. Cook for 1 1/2-2 mins each side until deep golden and crispy on the outside. Use a palette knife to lift the waffle away from the pan and set aside to cool while you make the second one.

  3. STEP 3 Roll the waffles into a cone shape and put in a glass to stand upright. Fill each one with a few scoops of ice cream, drizzle over the sauce and scatter over the raspberries.

Ingredients

  • 120g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour 1 tbsp custard powder
  • 100g golden caster sugar 2 large eggs , beaten
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil , plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract 50ml evaporated milk
