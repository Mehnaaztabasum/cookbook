Besan Halwa

Method

  1. Step 1 To start with, place a deep-bottomed pan over medium flame and boil 4 cups of water in it. Meanwhile, grind cardamom into a fine powder. Add in sugar, cardamom powder, saffron and stir until all the sugar granules get dissolved and syrup is formed.

  2. Step 2 Now, place another pan on medium flame and melt ghee in it. Once the ghee is hot enough, lower the flame and add unshelled pistachios and almonds in it and saute for two minutes. Mix in gram flour and stir until the colour turns light brown, stir continuously so as not burn the gram flour.

  3. Step 3 Pour the prepared sugar syrup in roasted gram flour and mix well till a thick consistency is achieved. Let the halwa cook for about two minutes and switch off the burner.

  4. Step 4 Mix in the rose water and stir once again. Serve warm on cold nights. (Optional: You can garnish the halwa with mixed dry fruits.)

Ingredients

  • 4 cup gram flour (besan) 2 tablespoon almonds
  • 2 tablespoon rose water
  • 1/2 teaspoon green cardamom 4 cup water 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoon pistachios 5 strand saffron
  • 2 cup ghee
