Hot chocolate

Method

  1. STEP 1 Heat the milk, cocoa, sugar and chocolate in a small pan over a medium heat until steaming and the chocolate has melted. Whisk to dissolve the cocoa.

  2. STEP 2 Pour into a mug, then add the cream to float on top. Grate over a little more chocolate to serve.

  3. You can visit You tube link also..here click the above link to open Hot chocolate

Ingredients

  • 250ml milk of your choice
  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 1-2 tbsp soft light brown sugar
  • 25g dark or plain chocolate, finely chopped
  • a grating to serve
  • 1 tbsp whipped cream or squirty cream
